# Communication & Training Hub

## Purpose
Help every team member know what matters for the current shift.

## Core outcomes
- Better shift communication
- Better menu knowledge
- Better training consistency
- Better certification tracking
- Better sales through better knowledge

## Included capabilities
- lineup notes
- menu updates
- recipe library
- training modules
- staff certifications

## UX principles
- mobile first
- fast to load
- easy to scan
- manager input, staff consumption
- shift context first
- avoid clutter

## MVP views
- Today's Shift Overview
- Lineup Notes Feed
- Menu Updates Panel
- Recipe Library
- Training Modules
- Certifications

## Important product constraints
- historical logging matters
- do not overwrite menu change history
- do not treat training as a bloated LMS
- support multiple locations later
